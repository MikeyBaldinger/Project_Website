About the Site

Most NFL databases provide an excessive amount of statistics. In reality, people only care about the skill players: quarterbacks, running backs, wide receivers, and tight ends. This web application only provides key statistics (eg. passing yards, rushing yards, touchdowns, etc.) for those skill players. This is to allow fantasy owners the opportunity to review a particular player's stats from the past season to make an informed decision for the upcoming season. This database allows such owners to obtain the important statistics they are looking for quickly and efficiently.
